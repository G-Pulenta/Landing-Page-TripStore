What is this?

“Trip Store” is an online platform that connects travelers with international buyers who are looking for exclusive products that are difficult to find in their countries of origin. The concept behind the platform is simple: shoppers request components or peripherals that are unavailable or too expensive in their country of origin, and travelers planning to relocate to that country offer to buy and carry the requested products. In this way, shoppers gain access to exclusive products at more affordable prices and travelers can earn additional money during their trips.
